# Parts List

This page lists all the essential parts needed to build the drone project:

#wh Notice
#wb Availability and specifications may vary depending on your location.

## Drone Hub

- **[Raspberry Pi Zero 2 W](https://www.amazon.de/Raspberry-Pi-Zero-2-W/dp/B09KLVX4RT)** (~23€): The main controller for managing communication and AI tasks.

## The Drones

- **[AzDelivery ESP32-C3 Microcontroller](https://www.amazon.de/-/en/DUBEUYEW-ESP32-C3-Development-Bluetooth-Compatible/dp/B0F65P46HZ)** (~22€ for 6): Controls the drone's operations.
- **[Happy Model SE 0802 Motors (16000KV, 4x)](https://de.aliexpress.com/item/1005007502560009.html)** (~30€ for 4): Lightweight and efficient motors.
- **[BETAFPV BT2.0 550mAh 1S Battery](https://de.aliexpress.com/item/1005008775642172.html)** (~32€ for 4): Provides power to the drone.
- **[LDARC 65mm 2-Blade Propellers (20pcs)](https://de.aliexpress.com/item/1005009401177467.html)** (~5€): Propellers for stable flight.
- **[Micro 1S 4A Brushless ESC](https://de.aliexpress.com/item/1005006197408875.html)** (~10€ for 4): Controls motor speed.
- **[GY-521 MPU6050](https://de.aliexpress.com/item/1005009131270695.html)** (~1€): Gyroscope sensor for stability.
- **[Mini 5V 0.5A BEC Regulator](https://de.aliexpress.com/item/1005001303802541.html)** (~2€): Converts power for the microcontroller.
- **[Mini PDB Power Distribution Board](https://de.aliexpress.com/item/1005004096995692.html)** (~5€): Distributes power to components.

## Miscellaneous

- **[Carbon Fibre PLA](https://eu.elegoo.com/de-at/products/pla-cf-filament-1-75mm-black-1kg)** (~22€): Lightweight filament for 3D printing.
- **[BetaFPV Battery Charger](https://de.aliexpress.com/item/1005009138071014.html)** (~25€): Charges the drone batteries.
