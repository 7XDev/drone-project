## Safety Precautions

### Do's:

#### Pre-Flight Checks
 - Inspect the drone for any damage or loose components before each flight.
 - Ensure the battery is fully charged and securely connected.
 - Verify that all propellers are properly attached and undamaged.

#### Safe Flying Practices
 - Always maintain a clear line of sight with the drone during operation.
 - Avoid flying near people, animals, or sensitive areas like airports.
 - Do not operate the drone in adverse weather conditions, such as strong winds or heavy rain.
